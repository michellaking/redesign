Taking Care of your Stainless Steel Jewelry

Maintaining Stainless Steel Jewelry

  1. Clean Regularly: Wipe with a soft cloth and mild soap to maintain shine. Avoid abrasive cleaners.
  2. Store Safely: Keep in a soft pouch or jewelry box to prevent scratches and dust buildup.
  3. Watch Chemicals: Avoid exposing your jewelry to harsh chemicals like bleach, which can cause discoloration.
  4. Stay Dry: Remove before swimming or showering to prevent tarnishing from water exposure.
  5. Polish Occasionally: If it loses its shine, gently polish with a soft cloth or stainless steel polishing cloth.

Cleaning Stainless Steel Jewelry

  1. Soap Solution: Mix warm water with mild soap.
  2. Scrub Gently: Soak your jewelry and softly scrub away dirt.
  3. Rinse: Wash off soap residue with clean water.
  4. Dry: Carefully dry with a soft cloth to prevent water spots.
  5. For Tough Stains: Use a stainless steel cleaner as directed.

What to Avoid When Cleaning Stainless Steel

  1. Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Say no to bleach or abrasive cleaners.
  2. Skip Abrasive Materials: Don't use steel wool or rough scrubbers.
  3. Watch Extreme Temperatures: Keep away from extreme heat or cold.
  4. Go Easy on Ultrasonic Cleaners: They can damage gemstones.
  5. Protect from Physical Impact: Take off during sports or heavy activities.
  6. Store Wisely: Keep in a cool, dry place away from sunlight and moisture.
